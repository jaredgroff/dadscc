About

Dad's Coffee Concoctions is a collection of some of the finest coffee recipes around.

Our story begins in 2016 in the humble home of a dad named Jared. It was here that the very first experimental concoctions were created using fresh coffee beans, various creamers, and a can-do attitude. The run-of-the-mill coffee brew just wasn't cutting it anymore. Jared was looking for more out of his morning coffee experience -- he was a dad now.

Seven years later, that dad has more kids and needs more coffee.

Recipes

Ingredients

Servings: 1

Prep time: 10 min

  • 6-8 oz of cold water
  • 1 tablespoon ground coffee
  • 2 oz sweetened condensed milk
  • 5 large ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Brew the coffee.
  2. Combine the brewed coffee and sweetened condensed milk into a large cup.
  3. Stir thoroughly until the coffee and condensed milk are blended together.
  4. Refrigerate until the coffee is chilled.
  5. Once chilled; pour in glasses filled with ice cubes.

Ingredients

Servings: 9

Prep time: 10 min

  • 1 ½ cups dark roast coffee, medium coarse ground
  • 6 cups room temperature filtered water
  • Milk and/or flavorings, optional for serving
  • Equipment: Large covered jar, strainer, and cheesecloth

Instructions

  1. Grind coffee to a medium coarse grind.
  2. Add water and coffee grounds to a large jar, close lid, and shake to mix.
  3. Refrigerate for 24 hours.
  4. Place strainer over a clean jar and line it with cheesecloth. Strain the coffee into the clean jar.
  5. To serve, dilute ½ cup cold brew concentrate with ½ cup water.
  6. Add milk as desired.

Ingredients

Servings: 1

Prep time: 10 min

  • 2 espresso shots (2 ounces)
  • 4 ounces (½ cup) fresh whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar and/or syrup for flavoring

Instructions

  1. Brew the espresso and pour into a mug.
  2. Froth the milk and pour into the center of the espresso.
  3. Add sugar to milk and espresso.

Ingredients

Servings: 1

Prep time: 10 min

  • 2 espresso shots (2 ounces)
  • 2 ounces (¼ cup) whole milk foam
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter

Instructions

  1. Brew the espresso and pour into a mug.
  2. Add the peanut butter, milk, and sugar to a saucepan. Heat, while stirring to melt the peanut butter. Bring the mixture to an almost boil.
  3. Froth the mixture and pour into the center of the espresso.
  4. Spoon the foam on top.

Ingredients

Servings: 1

Prep time: 10 min

  • 2 cups ice
  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee (cooled)
  • ¾ cup milk (or half and half)
  • ¼ cup caramel sauce
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • whipped cream and caramel sauce

Instructions

  1. Make 1 cup of strong brewed coffee
  2. Cover and chill in refrigerator for 15 minutes
  3. Put ice, cooled coffee, half & half, caramel sauce, sugar, and vanilla into a blender. Mix on high until smooth.
  4. Pour into glasses. Top with whipped cream and a drizzle of caramel sauce

Ingredients

Servings: 1

Prep time: 10 min

  • 2 scoops vanilla ice cream, (or vanilla gelato)
  • 2 oz espresso, (2 shots), or strong brewed coffee
  • Chocolate shavings or other toppings if desired

Instructions

  1. Brew 2 shots of espresso.
  2. Add 2 scoops of ice cream to a serving bowl or glass.
  3. Add chocolate shavings or other toppings if desired.
  4. Pour 2 shots of hot espresso over the ice cream and serve right away.
